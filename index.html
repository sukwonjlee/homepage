HELLO!

I am a Ph.D. student specializing in the area of Human Factors in the School of Industrial Engineering at Purdue University. I am a member of the HIVE Lab and pursuing my research under the supervision of Prof. Ji Soo Yi. I received a B.S. and a M.E. degrees in Industrial Engineering from Korea University in 2007 and 2009 respectively. Before joining the HIVE Lab, I served in the military as a first lieutenant of Republic of Korea Army and taught cadets in the Department of Weapon Systems Engineering at Korea Military Academy from 2009 to 2012.
